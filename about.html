▶ What Is BGMI?

Battlegrounds Mobile India

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is a battle royale game developed by Krafton Inc., exclusively for Indian players. Up to 100 players parachute onto a shrinking map, scavenge for weapons, and fight to be the last squad standing in intense, fast-paced matches.

BGMI has rapidly grown into one of India's most-played mobile games, spawning a thriving esports ecosystem. The BGMI Pro League was established to give grassroots squads a structured, professional competitive pathway — from local qualifiers all the way to national finals.

Whether you're a casual team looking to test your mettle or a seasoned squad chasing prize money and recognition, our platform offers a home for every level of competitor.
